## 11. **What is Regression in Minitab?**
**Answer:**  
Regression identifies relationships between independent variables and a response variable. Minitab provides linear, multiple, and polynomial regression models.

---

## 12. **What is multicollinearity and how do you detect it?**
**Answer:**  
Multicollinearity occurs when predictors are strongly correlated. Minitab identifies it using **VIF (Variance Inflation Factor)** values.

---

## 13. **Explain Capability Analysis.**
**Answer:**  
Capability analysis determines how well a process meets customer specification limits using metrics like:
- **Cp, Cpk** (Short-term performance)  
- **Pp, Ppk** (Long-term performance)  

---

## 14. **Difference between Cp and Cpk?**
**Answer:**  
- **Cp:** Checks potential capability assuming centered process  
- **Cpk:** Checks actual capability considering process shift  

---
